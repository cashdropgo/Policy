CashDrop GO - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CashDrop GO ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application CashDrop GO (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture when you create an account.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location to show nearby banknotes on the map and enable the drop/collect features. Location is only accessed while the app is in use.
  • Camera Data: Camera access for the AR scanning feature to find virtual banknotes. We do not store photos or videos.
  • Payment Information: PayPal email address for deposits and withdrawals. We do not store credit card or bank details.
  • Usage Data: Game activity including banknotes dropped, collected, and betting history.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for analytics and ad delivery.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (map, AR, wallet).
  • To process transactions and manage your virtual wallet.
  • To display nearby banknotes based on your location.
  • To show personalized advertisements (for free-tier users).
  • To communicate updates, promotions, and support.
  • To detect fraud and ensure fair gameplay.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal: To process deposits and withdrawals. See PayPal's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Authentication: For social login. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / CartoDB: For map tiles. No personal data is shared.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use encryption for sensitive data, secure authentication tokens (JWT), and industry-standard security measures.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

CashDrop GO is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to: access, correct, or delete your personal data; opt out of marketing; request a copy of your data; withdraw consent.

9. Advertising

Free-tier users see Google AdMob ads. Premium subscribers ($4.99/month or $39.99/year) enjoy ad-free experience.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.
